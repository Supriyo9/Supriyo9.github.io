Our Privacy Policy

No user data or any other data related to P Droid will ever be sold or made available to third parties. With P Droid, what happens on your device stays on your device.

P Droid does not send any data, anywhere, unless it is requested by the user (e.g. sending an automatic SMS.)

App Permissions : -

1.Contact permission: Possibility to easily select and add contacts within the app (No data will be collected or shared).

2.Notification access: Reads notifications in order to reply to them (No data will be collected or shared).

Retrieve running apps : To examine is user are using other apps while planting a tree

Run at start-up: For the "Phone Usage" features to work properly,if user restart their phone, the phone usage tracking system will automatically start running in the background

P Droid can make use of your Google Gmail account (if granted access) to send emails on your behalf via the send email action. P Droid will never use this access to send emails other than those configured by the user (using the send email action). Aside from the usage described, P Droid will not access, store or share any Google user data or account information.

No user data or any other data related to P Droid will ever be sold or made available to third parties.

P Droid will never include any third party services whose purpose is to monetize user data in any way.
